# E-COMMERCE-SALES-PERFORMANCE-ANALYSIS

📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data using SQL to uncover insights related to profitability, shipping efficiency, delivery performance, and sales trends. The objective is to identify business opportunities, improve operational efficiency, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

🎯 Business Questions

1. Which product categories are performing below and above the overall business profit margin?
2. How does shipping cost affect profit margins across different shipping modes?
3. Which sub-categories are causing margin leakage within low-performing categories?
4. Does longer delivery time negatively impact profitability or priority orders?
5. What are the yearly and monthly trends in sales, profit, and profit margin?

---

🛠️ Tools Used

 🗄️ Dataset Information

- Business Performance Analysis

 📈 Key Insights
- Identified product categories performing above and below the overall business profit margin.
- Analyzed the relationship between shipping costs and profit margins across shipping modes.
- Detected sub-categories causing margin leakage within low-performing categories.
- Evaluated the impact of delivery time on profitability and order priorities.
